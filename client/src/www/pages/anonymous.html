Quick Submit allows you to join with an automatically generated user ID and password. This identity will be saved in your browser until:

  • Your browser's cache is cleared.
  • You set or change the username, password, or email for your account.

As a result, we highly recommend that you join with your own identity and email address. In the case that you lose your login information as an anonymous user, you will not be able to recover your account due to the lack of an email.

You can convert any anonymous account to a standard account simply by adding an email address. Anonymous users may also be required to solve a reCaptcha when submitting data in the future.
